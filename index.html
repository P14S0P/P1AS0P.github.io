Installation & Requirements

Simple Installation

To install this modpack, simply download the CurseForge or Modrinth app, search for "Pingaland," make sure is the one made by: Piasop. Download it and launch it directly. The pack comes pre-configured and ready to play.

System Requirements

  • RAM: At least 4GB
  • CPU/GPU: Low-end friendly
  • Connectivity: Internet required for multiplayer
  • App: CurseForge to install and launch
